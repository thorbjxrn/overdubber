Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Overdubber is designed with your privacy in mind. Here is how the app handles your data:

Data We Collect

Overdubber does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All audio recordings and projects are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Advertising

The free version of Overdubber displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data for ad personalization as described in the Google Privacy Policy. Premium users do not see ads.

In-App Purchases

Overdubber offers a one-time Premium purchase processed through Apple's App Store. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Analytics

Overdubber does not use any third-party analytics services. We do not track how you use the app.

Data Storage

All recordings, projects, and settings are stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all associated data.

Children's Privacy

Overdubber does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
